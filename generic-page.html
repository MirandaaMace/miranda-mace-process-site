Design Challenge

This exercise is

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

Now, Frame it as a design question.

How would a website for The HEARTest Yard help raise awareness for CHD and help CHD children?

What is the impact you're trying to have?

Increase in donations and participation in events to help children with CHDs in Charlotte.

What are some possible solutions to the problem?

An easily accessible and approachable website. Appeal to donors, participants, and heart families and provide information.

Context and Restraints?

It’s a small organization that partners with Levine Children’s Hospital in CLT and with the Greg Olson Foundation; R4R. It might be difficult to get information from them because it is spread across several groups and organizations. Also, Greg Olson is moving to a different football team outside of CLT which could impact the organization.

Restating the Design Question.

How would a website for The HEARTest Yard provide accessible information to donors, participants, and heart families?
