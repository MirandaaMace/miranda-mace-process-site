Audience & Goals

After conducting research, I identified an audience for my website along with goals that the website is trying to achieve.

Audience

To figue out the audience that the website should be tailored towards, I completed this sticky note exercise to help visualize the groups of people.

I used sticky notes to visually arrange the groups. I originally had a third blue sticky note that said “Website Viewers” but after moving things around over and over I determined that that sticky note didn’t quite fit.

Under Event Participants I listed six groups. Friends of heart families, heart families, hospital staff, volunteers, runners, and supporters. These are the people who will actively participate in the community.

Under Donors and Sponsors, I listed three groups of people which included; sports fans of Greg Olsen, Companies, and People who have heard about the organization through social media or the news or by other means.

Overarching Goals

Client

  • Raise money.
  • Get sponsorships and donors.
  • Attract volunteers and Runners for 5k.
  • Create a community and bring heart families and friends together.
  • Raise awareness for children with congenital heart defects and the issues they face.

Audience

  • Become sponsors and donors.
  • Sign up to run in the 5k.
  • Sign up to volunteer for the 5k.
  • Become a part of the community.
  • Participate in other events.

Website

  • Provide an easy place to donate.
  • Offer easy access to race and event info.
  • Offer information on organization so donors know what their money is going towards.

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Donors and Sponsors Donate money, learn more about mission and goals to know where money is going. Donate/Sponsor, Learn More.
Runners for 5k Sign up for the race and get more information on the race and what the cause they are running for. Sign Up, Race info, Learn More.
Volunteers for 5k Sign up to volunteer and get more info on the race. Sign Up, Race Info, Learn More.
Friends of Heart kids, nurses, Supporters Getting involved in the community and getting info about upcoming events. Community, Donate/Sponsor, Learn More.
Sports Fans/Curious People Possibly donate, Learn about Greg Olsen’s involvement and the goals of the organization, Read real stories of families. Learn More, Donate/Sponsor, become a part of the community.

Conclusion

Overall, the goal through this website is to raise money for the cause through donors and events while growing the community. Through the funds, The HEARTest Yard can help children with their educational needs while they are stuck in the hospital. The audience's goal is to donate or register for an event while becoming a part of the community. Through joining forces with The HEARTest Yard, the audience will be making a difference while becoming a part of something bigger than themselves.

Through my research, I have come to the conclusion that The HEARTest Yard will be able to achieve more of their goals through having a website of their own. All of their information will be in one place, the audience will be able to get a clear idea of what the organization stands for as well as all the information involved in becoming a part of the community.
